ABOUT US

Conveniently located in downtown Liberal, Kansas, EasyEats was opened in 1995 after Chris Pirlo emigrated from Italy.

With several of his children supporting him in the kitchen, this legend still cooks at the age of 82. Many of the ingredients are imported from Italy and forms a taste that can't be replicated. In his piping hot retro oven, Dom has each mouthwatering pie ready to be devoured in just minutes.
